Dorm Info

Dort And Gold

One of the nicest living options on campus, dort and gold is an apartment style dorm traditonally set aside for upperclassmen. A spacious living room and double bathroom make it the largest suites available on campus

Amenities

- 4 Bedrooms
- 2 Bathrooms
- Kitchenette (Full Fridge, Microwave, Oven)
- dog park

Cost

$10,000 per semester

Pros

- Commual balcony
- Next to gym
- Dorm Parking Lot
- Lots of really nice apposums (Only marsupial native to US)

Cons

- Isolated from rest of dorms/social spaces
- Parking Fills up quick
- No indoor common spaces

Y/X/W/V

Our beautiful letter dorms! Home to the most common sized suite on campus, centrally located between HAM (Dining hall) and The Gym! Previously home to our living learning communities

Amenities

- 4 Bedrooms
- 1 Bathrooms
- Full Sized Fridge
- Vanity & Kitchen Sink
- Spacious Indoor Common Areas

Cost

$9,700 per semester

Pros

- Large balcony facing the nook
- Close to Gym/Ham/Parking
- Dorm Parking Lot (Next to Y dorm)
- Piano/Computer Lab with printers/Meditation Space

Cons

- Thin walls
- Not a ton of privacy
- Super messy (depending on letter)

Z Dorm

Z dorm is a very sought after dorm on campus, with large swaths of indoor communal spaces as well as spacious outdoor areas

Amenities

- 4 Bedrooms
- 1 Bathrooms
- Amphitheater
- Three Story Balcony (Zalcony)
- High density of lizards

Cost

$8,450 per semester

Pros

- HUGE 3 Story balcony beloved by students
- Right across from dining hall
- Amphitheater for live music
- Ocasional Racoon

Cons

- Very little snakes
- Some rooms are better then others
- Elevator breaks 24/7
