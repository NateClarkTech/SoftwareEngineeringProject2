Welcome to NuPath

NuPath is a site dedicated to helping new students at the New College of Forida adapt to this new environment, and stay informed of important dates, contact other Novos, Keep track of closing times, and food quality.

App Features

  • Calendar: Keep track of your important dates, deadlines, and events.
  • NCF Hours: Keep track of importat opening and closing times for many organizations on campus.
  • Don't Kill Me Food: Keep track of which days the food may be lethal on campus.
  • Organizations: Connect with student organizations and become a part of the campus community.
  • Personal Profiles: Customize your profile, See others, and interact with your fellow students.
  • Chat: Chat with other students at New College.
  • Leaderboard/Checklist Keep track of your progress on the New College checklist and leaderboard, mark things as done to gain points!.
  • Shopping List: Find must haves for your life here at new college.
